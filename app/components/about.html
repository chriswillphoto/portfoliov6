A little about me

I just like to make things. It's always been that way. Stacking lego, throwing paint at a canvas, or better yet - my bedroom walls (sorry Mum!), playing with cameras or nailing blocks of wood together for no apparent reason; I revel in the act of creative expression and I live to solve a problem.

The projects displayed here were created during my time as a Web Development student at General Assembly, where I put my affinity for making and problem solving to good use. Over a 3 month, full time course, I made things every day. I explored, I experimented and I loved every minute of it.

Perhaps I could make things for you?
